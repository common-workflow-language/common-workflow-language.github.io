Health and Safety

The event has a Health and Safety policy (inspired by publichealthpledge.com).

If the policy changes it is only strengthened - never weakened - between the event's announcement and the event itself.

  1. In-person participants will be require to wear a FFP3 face mask.
  2. Any scheduled meals and coffee breaks will be outside.
  3. Attendees will be expected to perform a rapid COVID-19 test prior to participating each day.
  4. The CO2 level in the rooms will be monitored to ensure good ventilation (PPM under 1000).
